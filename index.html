About Me

Hi! I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Jiaheng Zhang. I am interested at the intersection of security, applied cryptography, machine learning, blockchain, and high performance computing. In my undergraduate years, I worked with wonderful people including Prof. Dawn Song, Prof. Neil Gong, Prof. Chao Zhang. I also interned at Microsoft Research Asia.
