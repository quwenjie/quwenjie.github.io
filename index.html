About Me

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the National University of Singapore, fortunately advised by Prof. Jiaheng Zhang. I had the privilege of working with Prof. Dawn Song (UC Berkeley), Prof. Neil Gong (Duke University), and Prof. Chao Zhang (Tsinghua University). I have also interned at Microsoft Research.

My research broadly spans AI security and applied cryptography, with a focus on building secure, verifiable, and auditable AI systems.

I am always open to collaborations. If you share similar interests, feel free to email me.
