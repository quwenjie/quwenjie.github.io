About Me

Hi! I recently received my bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Currently I am an research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, Trustworthy Systems group. I am an incoming Computer Science PhD student at National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Jiaheng Zhang. I am interested in the intersection of security, machine learning, blockchain, and applied cryptography. In my undergraduate years, I worked with wonderful people including Prof. Dawn Song, Prof. Neil Gong, Prof. Chao Zhang and Prof. Binghui Wang.
