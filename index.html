About Me

Hi! I am a senior undergraduate student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dawn Song at UC Berkeley as a research intern. I have also worked closely with Prof. Neil Gong at Duke University, Prof. Chao Zhang at Tsinghua University and Professor Binghui Wang at Illinois Institute of Technology. I am broadly interested in various topics in computer security including applied cryptography and AI security. I am a recipient of the National Scholarship(2020). I will be applying for CS PhD in US in Fall 2023, feel free to contact me.
